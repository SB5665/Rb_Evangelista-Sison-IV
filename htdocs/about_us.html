■What are vtubers?

The word vtuber derives from the words "Virtual" & "Youtuber". These Vtubers are online entertainers with massive followings. Vtubers are also not exclusive to only Youtube but they can also be found in a plethora of other social media sites like Twitch, Facebook Live, Bilibili, etc. They may be affiliated with a group or a company, or they can opt to be an indie. Major comapnies and groups in the vtubing world are "Nijisanji", "Hololive", "Vshoujo", "KizunaAI", just to name a few.
