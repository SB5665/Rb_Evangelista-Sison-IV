■What are vtubers?

The word vtuber derives from the words "Virtual" & "Youtuber". These Vtubers are online entertainers with massive followings. They may be affiliated with a group or a company, or the can opt to be an indie. Major comapnies and groups in the vtubing world are "Nijisanji", "Hololive", "Vshoujo", "KizunaAI", and many more.
