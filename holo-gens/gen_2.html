Minato Aqua is a marine-styled virtual maid with the personality of an airhead. She is childish, and throws tantrums quite easily. She also loves to play pranks in games, although most of them backfire on them. In highly technical games, however, she is very skilled, hitting high ranks in those games. She does have an interior however, where she is kind, responsible, and actually introverted.

Murasaki Shion is a smug and cheeky dark magician, with the "kusogaki" title given to her due to it. She is typically impolite, and loves flirting with her peers. She wishes to be treated as an adult, although she looks silly whenever she tries to act like one. She has a rivalry with Minato Aqua, where they pull harmless pranks on each other.

Nakiri Ayame is a kimono-clad oni(evil spirit/demon) girl who loves to play pranks and thinks of people below her, although she is actually very friendly and changes her mind, thinking of her audience as her equals. She is very approachable and laughs very easily. She does love analyzing new people however, where she collects data first before talking to the person face to face.

Yuzuki Choco is the school nurse at the Underworld Academy, being incredibly popular especially with the boys. She blurs the line between being motherlike and seductive, especially with her voice. She is also sweet like her name, and also loves sweets. She also cares for her viewers' health and wellbeing, although she has does have a morbid sense of humor.

Oozora Subaru is a manager of both a Mixed Martial Arts team and an e-sports club with an extroverted and tomboyish personality. She is confident, hardworking and very optimistic. She has gone through very overwhelming life experiences, having a hole in her heart and having her house burn down, which led to her pointing out ridiculousness so something like those wouldn't happen again, although she isn't incapable of smiling.
